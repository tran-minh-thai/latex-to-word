\section{Introduction}
Association rule mining discovers frequent itemsets and rules in transaction
databases \cite{Agrawal1994}. The Apriori principle, namely that every subset of a
frequent itemset is frequent, enables effective candidate pruning; later methods
such as FP-growth avoid candidate generation altogether \cite{Han2000}. This note
summarises a pruning-based formulation in Section~\ref{sec:method}.
